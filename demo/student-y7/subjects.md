## Core Subjects

- [ ] English — Ms. Chen
  tags: english, core
  priority: high
  **Current unit:** Persuasive writing and argument structure
  **Next unit:** Australian poetry and identity
  **Assessment:** Persuasive essay (Week 5), Book report (Week 6)
  **Goal:** Improve essay structure — use TEEL paragraphs consistently. Read more widely.

- [ ] Mathematics — Mr. Patel
  tags: maths, core
  priority: high
  **Current unit:** Algebra — variables, expressions, and equations
  **Next unit:** Geometry — area, perimeter, volume
  **Assessment:** Algebra test (Week 5), Geometry project (Week 8)
  **Goal:** Stop rushing through working out. Show every step to get full marks.

- [ ] Science — Ms. Walker
  tags: science, core
  priority: high
  **Current unit:** Earth and space sciences — ecosystems and human impact
  **Next unit:** Chemical sciences — mixtures and separating techniques
  **Assessment:** Ecosystems poster (Week 4), Lab report (Week 3)
  **Goal:** Write better discussion sections in lab reports. Link results back to hypothesis.

- [ ] HASS — Mr. Nguyen
  tags: hass, core
  priority: high
  **Current unit:** Ancient civilisations — Egypt and Mesopotamia
  **Next unit:** Geography — place and liveability
  **Assessment:** Egypt timeline (Week 6), Mapping assignment (Week 7)
  **Goal:** Include more evidence in source analysis. Don't just describe — analyse.

- [ ] HPE — Coach Davis
  tags: hpe, core
  priority: medium
  **Current unit:** Fitness components and personal health
  **Next unit:** Team sports — touch football and netball
  **Assessment:** Fitness journal (ongoing), Nutrition reflection (Week 3)
  **Goal:** Improve beep test score from 7.2 to 8.0 by end of term.
